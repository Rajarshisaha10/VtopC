A short, plain-English summary of how VtopC works and what you agree to when using it.

Last updated: July 12, 2026

Independent Student Project

VtopC is an independent, open-source student dashboard for viewing VTOP data in a cleaner interface. It is not an official VIT or VTOP product, and it is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or supported by Vellore Institute of Technology.

Privacy

VtopC uses your VTOP credentials only to log in to VTOP and fetch the academic data you request, such as attendance, marks, grades, timetable, exam schedule, profile, and room details. Session data is used to keep the dashboard working while you are signed in.

We do not sell your data, run ads, create marketing profiles, or share your academic data with advertisers. Some settings, cached dashboard data, and preferences may be stored in your browser so the app feels faster and remembers your choices.

Storage & Security

VtopC may use server-side sessions, cookies, browser local storage, and configured backend services only as needed to provide app features. You can log out and clear your browser storage or cookies at any time.

We try to handle data carefully, but no website can promise perfect security. Use VtopC only on trusted devices and networks, and keep your VTOP credentials private.

Responsible Use

Use VtopC only with your own VTOP account and for personal academic convenience. Do not use someone else's credentials, attempt unauthorized access, overload VTOP systems, bypass security controls, or use the app in a way that violates institutional rules or applicable law.

Accuracy & Availability

VtopC depends on VTOP, network access, and page formats that may change. Data may be delayed, unavailable, cached, incomplete, or parsed incorrectly. For official decisions, fees, exams, attendance, grades, deadlines, and records, always verify directly on the official VTOP portal or with the institution.
