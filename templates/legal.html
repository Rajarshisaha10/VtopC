Privacy Policy

VtopC is an independent student dashboard made by AIP. This Privacy Policy explains how the application handles information when you use it to access VTOP-related academic data.

Information We Process

When you log in, VtopC processes your VTOP username, password, CAPTCHA result, session identifier, academic records, timetable, attendance, marks, exam schedule, profile details, and related dashboard data needed to provide the service. We do not track users across sessions, create behavioral profiles, or share analytics with any third party beyond what third-party services handle under their own policies.

How Information Is Stored

VTOP session data is stored temporarily in server memory. Your browser's local storage stores settings, preferences, selected semester, cached dashboard data, and session identifiers—this data never leaves your device. Login credentials may be stored in an HttpOnly cookie to support optional auto-login. All stored data can be manually cleared anytime through your browser or within the app.

How Information Is Used

Information is used solely to authenticate with VTOP, fetch your academic data, display dashboard features, calculate attendance insights, maintain session continuity, and improve your user experience. We do not use data for profiling, marketing, or any purpose beyond providing the core service.

Analytics and Cookies

VtopC may use analytics services provided by third parties like Google and Vercel for performance monitoring and educational purposes only. These services operate under their own privacy policies. Analytics data is used to understand usage patterns and improve the application, not to track individual users. You can clear all cookies in your browser settings at any time.

Notifications and Background Processes

VtopC does not send push notifications, background alerts, or maintain background processes that monitor your behavior. All notifications are displayed within the app interface only when you're actively using it.

Third-Party Services

VtopC communicates with VTOP systems to retrieve your academic data. Some optional features may use services such as Firebase, Supabase, or other configured backends. These services process data according to their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review.

Data Sharing and Non-Monetization

VtopC is a free, non-commercial project. We do not sell, trade, or monetize any personal information. Data is sent only to VTOP and configured backend services as needed to deliver the features you've enabled. No data is shared with advertisers, marketing partners, or any other third parties for commercial purposes.

Open Source

VtopC is an open-source project created for learning and experimentation. The source code is publicly available for anyone to review, modify, and contribute to through the project's GitHub repository. Community contributions do not affect your privacy or transmit your data externally.

Your Privacy Choices

You can log out to clear the active server session and saved credentials. You can also clear your browser's local storage and cookies for this site to remove all cached data, preferences, and session information. These actions can be performed anytime through your browser settings or the app's interface.

Security

VtopC uses server-side sessions and HttpOnly cookies where applicable to protect your data. However, no system can guarantee absolute security. We recommend using VtopC only on trusted devices and networks, and you are responsible for keeping your VTOP credentials safe.

Disclaimer

VtopC is provided on an "as-is" basis with no guarantees of accuracy, reliability, or availability. We are not responsible for data inaccuracies, login failures, or service interruptions caused by VTOP updates or other external factors. Always verify critical information directly with VTOP or your institution.
